Target Audience

Our target audience is all those people with small and medium-sized companies who wish to start a business or strengthen it in the technology sector, as well as anyone interested in the technology sector who could be potential clients for these small and medium-sized companies.

Persona 1

Dermographic

  • Name: Eduardo Marin
  • Age: 21
  • Ocupation: Computer engineering student
  • Experience: Computer knowledge and software development at engineering level, one year of work experience.

Background

He is about to finish his studies, he is a fan of technology, he loves to participate in workshops and events related to technology. You want to start your own business but you don't have much information about it.

Scenarios

  • In his last year of study, he decides to look for relevant information on how to start a technology business.
  • Find information on the different services you can provide.
  • Find information about alliances with other companies and the type of investment to make.
  • Learn the process of how to start a new business, the requirements it must have and the type of investment to make.
  • Learn about the different types of technological entrepreneurship events and fairs throughout the year.

Persona 2

Dermographic

  • Name: Sherley Rodriguez
  • Age: 38
  • Ocupation:Software Developer
  • Experience: 10 year in web Design Developer

Background

She has worked for 10 years as a software developer and she want to start her own software development company, she has heard from the Chamber of Commerce and that it has very relevant information for entrepreneurship, but first she wants to train to be a full-stack developer..

Scenarios

  • Find information on training events as a software developer in web technology.
  • Find different types of recreational activities and entrepreneurship fairs in software development throughout the year.
  • Find out what are the requirements that she must reconcile to open a software development company in the locality.
  • She knows the different types of local software development companies, and what are the job benefits for employees.
  • Find partnership opportunity information with other local software development companies.
