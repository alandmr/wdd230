Hick’s Law

Farmatodo

farmatodo.com.ve

The Hick’s Law teaches us that when we have a web page in which they have a wide catalog of products, the filters come to be of great help to the user. This website is a from pharmacy that allows to customers purchasing products online. It is a perfect example of the Hick’s Law.

Fitt’s Law

Family Search

familysearch.org

Familysearch is a perfect example of the Fitt's Law, when we're entering to that website we observe that in each section of the page there is a part that invites you to action and the time that a user would take to move to the target area would be very minimal.

Alignment

The Church of Jesus Christ of latter-day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

The website of the Church of Jesus Christ shows a clear example of alignment of the elements, these elements are aligned both horizontally and vertically, allowing users to have a better experience when searching for the required information. This web page clearly shows the principle of alignment,.
